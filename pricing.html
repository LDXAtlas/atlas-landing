Common questions

Can I switch plans at any time?

Absolutely. You can upgrade your plan at any time, no penalties, no waiting periods. Your billing adjusts automatically at the start of your next cycle. Not sure which plan fits your church best? Our team is happy to help.

What happens when my trial ends?

At the start of your last week of the trial you will receive an email asking if you would like to sign up for one of our tiers. Upon signing up you will just continue to use the platform as you have been. If you choose not to, you will lose access once the trial period has ended. We will keep your account for one month once you cancel in case you change your mind.

Do I need tech experience to set up the platform?

Not at all. Atlas was built from the ground up for ministry leaders, not developers. If you can send an email, you can use Atlas. And if you ever get stuck, our real human support team is on standby. No bots, no ticket queues, no waiting.

How does Atlas secure your data?

Every piece of data in Atlas is encrypted using the same standard used by banks and healthcare providers. Your member information never leaves your organization, we don't sell it, share it, or use it for advertising. Ever. Data Privacy Framework →
