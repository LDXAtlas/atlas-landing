Common Questions

Do I need to be technically inclined to set Atlas up?

Not at all. We built Atlas specifically so that anyone, regardless of technical background, can get their entire church set up and running on the very first day. If you can send an email, you can use Atlas.

Does Atlas secure your private data?

Security is our highest priority. We use enterprise-grade encryption to protect all of your church's data, ensuring your member information, care notes, and communications remain completely private and secure.

Can I switch plans at any time?

Absolutely. You are never locked in. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time directly from your billing dashboard as your ministry grows or your needs change.
