Cookie Policy

Last Updated: 04/02/26

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website or use an app. They are widely used to make websites and apps work more efficiently and to provide information to the website or app owners.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on Atlas Church Solutions website and Services:

  • Essential/Necessary Cookies: These cookies are required for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as session management, user authentication, and security features. Without these cookies, the Services cannot operate properly.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide a more personalized experience. For example, they may remember your language preference or theme selection.
  • Analytical Cookies: These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website and Services. We use tools such as Google Analytics to collect and analyze usage data. This helps us improve our Services and understand user behavior patterns.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use, including: (a) Google Analytics for web analytics; (b) Advertising partners for retargeting and marketing. These third parties have their own cookie policies, and we are not responsible for their use of cookies.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways: (a) Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. Instructions for controlling cookies in popular browsers can be found at [www.allaboutcookies.org]; (b) Opt-Out Tools: You can opt-out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on; (c) Do Not Track: If your browser supports Do Not Track signals, you can enable this feature (though we do not currently respond to DNT signals).

5. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling cookies may impact the functionality of our Services. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without affecting your ability to use certain features.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy, please contact us at Info@atlaschurchsolutions.com.
